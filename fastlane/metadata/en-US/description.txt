Automatically monitor your clipboard in the background. ClipKit saves everything you copy and lets you instantly recall it from a widget or custom keyboard — with a first-time setup guide to get you started right away.

[Free Features]
• Background clipboard monitoring (using PiP technology)
• Auto-save up to 20 items
• Text, image & URL support
• iCloud sync (up to 20 items)
• First-time onboarding guide

[Pro Features]
• Unlimited history (up to 100 items)
• Advanced search
• Favorites & pin (instantly access your most-used clips)
• Home screen widgets (3 sizes)
• Custom keyboard
• 7-day free trial

ClipKit works around iOS 16+ clipboard restrictions using Picture-in-Picture, so monitoring continues even while you use other apps.
